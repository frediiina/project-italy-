Apulia

Apulia, or Puglia in Italian, is known for its rich culinary traditions and delicious regional dishes.

Orecchiette con Cime di Rapa - Little Ears pasta with Turnip Tops

This dish is very easy to make. The ingredients include:

  • Orecchiette
  • Garlic cloves
  • Red chillies
  • Anchovies
  • Broccoli rabe or tenderstem broccoli
  • Pecorino
