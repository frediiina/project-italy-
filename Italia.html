Lazio

Meals in Lazio are known for diverse flavors, a sophisticated sensibility, and the usage of local produce and meats.

Much of Lazio cuisine is characterized by cured pork meats, olive oil, herbs, pasta, anchovies, and fresh produce.

Cacio e Pepe - Cheese and pepper spaghetti

This dish is very easy to make. The ingredients include:

  • Pecorino Romano
  • Peppercorns
  • Butter
